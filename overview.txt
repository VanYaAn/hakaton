HACKATHON OVERVIEW: Productivity Code
===========================================

Challenge: Develop a service in MAX messenger to improve personal effectiveness

Event Details:
--------------
- Organizers: VK Education & MAX messenger
- Date: November 5, 2025
- Platform: MAX messenger
- Audience: 50+ million users (19+ million daily active users)

About MAX Messenger:
--------------------
- National messenger available since March 26, 2025
- Available in RuStore, AppStore, and Google Play
- Supports Russian and Belarusian SIM cards
- Growing rapidly with over 50M total users and 19M+ daily users

Opportunity:
------------
Be among the first to create chatbots and mini-applications in MAX messenger.
Create popular and convenient services for work, learning, communication, and life.

Task:
-----
Create a WORKING PROTOTYPE of a chatbot or chatbot with mini-application in MAX messenger
that solves an actual problem for youth audience related to improving productivity.

Key Requirements:
-----------------
1. Define target audience and the problem to solve
2. Design UX and interaction scenarios
3. Build MVP (Minimum Viable Product) - working chatbot or chatbot with mini-app in MAX
4. Prepare project presentation
5. Defend the project before expert jury in Moscow finals

Focus Areas for Productivity Services:
---------------------------------------
- Task planning: creating lists, deadline control, calendar integration
- Motivation: regular reminders, rewards for completed tasks, personalized recommendations
- Time management: Pomodoro technique, focus improvement, task prioritization
- Physical health tracking: activity logging, nutrition control, sleep and health monitoring
- Personal goals: tracking long-term goals like career development or health improvement

Target Users:
-------------
- Professionals: project managers, mid/senior level employees, entrepreneurs
- Students and youth: academic task planning, motivation, physical activity
- Personal development focused individuals: self-improvement, progress tracking
- Corporate clients: employee wellbeing and productivity improvement

Market Context:
---------------
- Global productivity app market expected to grow from $59.88B (2023) to $149.74B (2030)
- Popular apps: Todoist (30M users), Trello (50M), Notion (100M+), Headspace (65M), Strava (120M)
- Russian examples: Mail.ru (50M users in Russia), VK WorkSpace (20K+ corporate clients)

Developer Resources:
--------------------
- Developer documentation: https://dev.max.ru/docs/legal/rules
- Recommended: JavaScript and React (other languages allowed via API)
